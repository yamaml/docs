**YAMA** (circa 2019) is a family of human-friendly formats for creating, managing, and publishing metadata application profiles. Its current authoring language is YAMAML. A single YAMAML source document can produce RDF, SHACL, ShEx, OWL-DSP, SimpleDSP, DCTAP, diagrams, and Frictionless Data Packages.
